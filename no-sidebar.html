Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A Market Snapshot Package typically takes 2-3 weeks, the Competitive Edge Package takes 4-6 weeks, and the Go-to-Market Pro Package takes 8-12 weeks from kickoff to final delivery.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! While our three main packages cover most needs, we're happy to create custom solutions tailored to your specific requirements. Contact us to discuss your unique situation.

What industries do you serve?

We specialize in the low-FODMAP food industry, serving manufacturers, startups, retailers, and distributors. Whether you're producing packaged foods, supplements, or meal kits, we have the expertise to help.

Is there a free consultation?

Yes! We offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation to discuss your needs and determine how we can best support your business. There's no obligation, and it's a great way to get to know our approach.

What geographic markets do you cover?

We provide insights for North American markets as our primary focus, with capabilities to analyze international markets including Europe, Australia, and Asia-Pacific regions upon request.
